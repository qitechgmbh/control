### Start Sequence

1. Plug in power, connect USB.
2. Launch JARVIS Control software.
3. Mount spool and calibrate with laser.
4. Thread filament and configure puller speed.
5. Begin winding with weight arm.
6. Replace spool as needed.
7. Stop with standby/off modes or emergency stop.
8. Unplug for shutdown.
